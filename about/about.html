Welcome to Kamalpokhari Boys Hostel

Established in 2010, Kamalpokhari Boys Hostel has been providing affordable and comfortable accommodation to students from all over Nepal. Our mission is to create a home-like environment where students can focus on their studies while enjoying modern amenities and a supportive community.

Located just 5 minutes walk from Kamalpokhari, our hostel offers easy access to major educational institutions, shopping areas, and public transportation. We take pride in our clean facilities, healthy meals, and 24/7 security to ensure peace of mind for both students and their parents.
